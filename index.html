Welcome to Kai's Cosmos

Hi! I am Kai Wang, a senior undergraduate in the School of the Gifted Young (SCGY), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I was born in 1996 and grow up in a small and beatiful village in AnHui province.

I am majoring in Astrophysics, mainly focus on galaxy and cosmology. I was interested in CMB data analysis and inflation physics and have finished several projects under the guidance of Prof. Wen Zhao. Now, I have turned my attention to the physics of galaxy formation, which is less theoretical but more complex. I will go to Tsinghua Center for Astrophysics in Tsinghua University for my PhD program and my advisors are Prof. Cheng Li and Prof. Hou-Jun Mo.

I am interested in a board range of topics, from the theoretical calculation to observation and data analysis. Our Universe is complex, we cannot understand her with a few methods. To solve some problems and know our Universe well, we need to employ different tools, from paper to telescope, from pencil to computer.
